Bhopal - the city of lakes

Bhopal is the capital city of the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh and the administrative headquarters of both Bhopal district and Bhopal division. It is known as the City of Lakes for its various natural and artificial lakes and for being one of the greenest cities in India.

Historical Places

  • Sanchi Stupa

    • Sanchi is a Buddhist complex, famous for its Great Stupa, on a hilltop at Sanchi Town in Raisen District of the State of Madhya Pradesh, India. It is located in 46 kilometres north-east of Bhopal, capital of Madhya Pradesh. The Great Stupa at Sanchi is one of the oldest stone structures in India, and an important monument of Indian Architecture.

  • Bhojpur

    • The Bhojeshwar Temple is an incomplete Hindu temple in Bhojpur village of Madhya Pradesh, India. Dedicated to Shiva, it houses a 7.5 feet (2.3 m) high lingam in its sanctum. The temple's construction is believed to have started in the 11th century, during the reign of the Paramara king Bhoja.

  • Udayagiri Caves

    • The Udayagiri Caves are twenty rock-cut caves near Vidisha, Madhya Pradesh from the early years of the 5th century CE. They contain some of the oldest surviving Hindu temples and iconography in India. They are the only site that can be verifiably associated with a Gupta period monarch from its inscriptions.

    Lakes

  • Upper Lake

    • The Upper Lake was built in the 11th century by Raja Bhoj. A huge statue of Raja Bhoj, standing with sword, was also installed on a pillar on one corner of the lake to cement the name of Bhopal as the city of lakes. The centre of this lake also has a dargah of a local Muslim saint known as Shah Ali Shah.

  • Lower Lake

    • The Lower Lake is located in the old part of the city, and was built by Nawab Chhote Khan in 1794. Just in the middle of the city. On one side Kali Mandir and on the other side Professor Colony and Talyia places are located around this Lower Lake/ Chotta Talab.

  • Shahpura Lake

    • Charming place to visit in the Bhopal is the famous Shahpura lake. Manisha market situated just near this lake so it is called Manisha Lake also.

    Temples

  • Gufa Mandir

    • Gufa Mandir is really good to see. Shivlinga, Ganpati, radha krishna, Ram idols are there in this temple located in Gufa Mandir Road, Nayapura, Lalghati Bhopal,Madhya Pradesh State, India.

  • ManuaBhan tekri

    • The Manuabhan ki Tekri gathers much significance as it is the highest point of the capital city.There is a Jain temple perched atop this hill.The Hill commands lovely panoramic views of the Upper lake and the old bhopal town located in Lalghati Bhopal,MP,India

  • Birla Mandir

    • Birla mandir is a very old and beautiful temple in bhopal. The atmosphere in and around the temple is quite amazing. You can have a glimpse of beautiful lower lake from the back side of the temple located in Arera Hills, Bhopal, MP, India
